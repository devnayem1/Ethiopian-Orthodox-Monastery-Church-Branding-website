Welcome

A New Sacred
Home in California

✦

"From the ancient highlands of Ethiopia to the mountains of Northern California — faith takes root, and community flourishes."

Gishen Mariam Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Monastery and Church has been formally approved by the greater Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church in Sacramento. We are now establishing our early presence in Shasta County, California — a sacred home for worship, monastic life, cultural preservation, and community service.

Our property includes an existing church building with long-term vision for spiritual retreats, wellness programs, recovery support, and cultural programming for all who seek peace, healing, and spiritual nourishment.

Our Story

Rooted in Ancient Faith

From the highlands of Lalibela to the mountains of Northern California — a living tradition continues.

Ancient Roots

The Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church

One of the world's oldest Christian traditions, the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church traces its origins to the 4th century AD and the baptism of the Ethiopian eunuch described in Acts 8. For over 1,700 years, Ethiopian Christians have maintained their unique liturgical tradition, sacred music, and monastic heritage.

The Vision

A Sacred Gathering Place in California

Inspired by faith and the needs of the Ethiopian Orthodox community in Northern California, a group of faithful believers and clergy came together with a shared vision: to establish a monastery and church that would serve as a spiritual home, cultural anchor, and center of community life in Shasta County.

Official Formation

Approved by the Greater Church in Sacramento

After faithful prayer, planning, and proper canonical process, Gishen Mariam Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Monastery & Church has received formal approval from the greater Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church in Sacramento. This marks a historic moment for our community.

Today & Tomorrow

Building Our Sacred Home

With an existing church property in Shasta County, we are now in the vital early formation stage — establishing our public presence, building our community, and raising the funds needed to renovate, develop, and sustain this sacred place for generations to come. Your support makes this holy work possible.

Looking Forward

Our Vision for the Future

Gishen Mariam will grow into a comprehensive center of faith, healing, culture, and community service.

🏔

Spiritual Retreat Center

A sanctuary for silent retreats, prayer weeks, and spiritual direction for the faithful seeking deeper communion with God.

🌿

Wellness & Recovery

Holistic wellness and recovery programs supporting at-risk individuals through faith-based healing, counseling, and community care.

🤝

Community Outreach

Serving our neighbors regardless of background — food drives, family support, youth mentorship, and partnerships with local organizations.

🎶

Cultural Programming

Celebrating and preserving Ethiopian heritage through language classes, traditional music, iconography workshops, and cultural festivals.

📖

Theological Education

Classes in Ge'ez, Orthodox theology, liturgical studies, and Ethiopian church history — nurturing the next generation of leaders.

🎵

Kidus Yared Hall

Named for Saint Yared, father of Ethiopian sacred music — a future music center honoring the living tradition of Ethiopian liturgical song and sacred arts.
